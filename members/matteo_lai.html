Matteo's doctoral research project is "A deep learning framework for unveiling multimodal neural signatures in the Autism Spectrum Disorder".

He is focused on explaining the continuum of autism spectrum disorder and identifying potential biomarkers by employing state-of-the-art deep learning methodologies, such as Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs), to analyze neuroimaging, EEG, and clinical data.
